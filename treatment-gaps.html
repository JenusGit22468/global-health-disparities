Methodology & Data Sources

Our treatment gap analysis is based on comprehensive data from multiple authoritative sources:

  • WHO Mental Health Atlas 2023: Service coverage and utilization data
  • IHME Global Burden of Disease Study: Disease prevalence estimates
  • World Bank: Healthcare expenditure and resource allocation
  • Peer-reviewed literature: Country-specific studies and surveys

Treatment gap is calculated as the percentage of people with mental health conditions who do not receive adequate treatment within a 12-month period.

Confidence Intervals

All estimates include 95% confidence intervals. Treatment gap calculations follow WHO standardized methodology with adjustment for regional variations in service delivery models.

Treatment Gap Calculation

Treatment Gap (%) =
((Total Need - People Treated) / Total Need) × 100

Where Total Need = Population × Prevalence Rate × Severity Adjustment Factor

Economic Analysis Methodology

Economic Cost Categories

  • Direct Medical Costs: Healthcare utilization, medications, hospitalization
  • Direct Non-Medical Costs: Transportation, informal care, social services
  • Indirect Costs: Lost productivity, absenteeism, premature mortality
  • Intangible Costs: Quality of life, pain and suffering (QALY/DALY)

Data Sources

  • WHO Global Health Expenditure Database
  • World Bank National Accounts Data
  • ILO Labour Force Statistics
  • IHME Global Burden of Disease Study

Economic Burden Calculation

Total Economic Burden =
Direct Costs + Indirect Costs + Intangible Costs

Direct = Healthcare + Social Services
